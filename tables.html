Random Generators

Below are some generators that I have created. While the Skazka campaign setting is based on the Dungeons & Dragons 5E rules (2014) many of the generators could be used for other RPG games or settings too.

Please report issues you find here. I'd also welcome your homebrew subbmissions. If you can't code, don't worry; I can take your idea and make it a reality.

Herbalism

A herb generator which links directly to the gathering house rules used in my Skazka campaign setting, but no reason it cannot be used for other game worlds. Each herb is medicinal, toxin or an enhancer. Some herbs can be both medicinal and an enhancer; others can be a toxin and an enhancer. Consume wisely.

Gathering house rules.

Foraging

A foraging generator which links directly to the gathering house rules used in my Skazka campaign setting, but no reason it cannot be used for other game worlds. Adventurers need to eat and this aspect of RPGs is often overlooked. This table (and my house rules) make foraging easy..

Gathering house rules.

Hunting

A hunting generator which links directly to the gathering house rules used in my Skazka campaign setting, but no reason it cannot be used for other game worlds. Generate the creature hunted based on your location. The Yield returned tells you how many meals that will feed you for. It also hints at what you can attempt to skin.

Gathering house rules.
