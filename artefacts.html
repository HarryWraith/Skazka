The Tears of Uzume

Introduction

When the goddess Uzume was slain by Fafnirog, her death cry shook the heavens. From that cry spilled her sorrow, her rage, her joy, and her final dreams. These emotions fell as shards of crystal across Skazka in the celestial storm known as the Starfall.

The world calls them the Tears of Uzume: thumb-sized gems of otherworldly radiance—indestructible, and forever humming with divine memory. To hold one is to clutch a fragment of a goddess’ last breath.

How They Work

  • Socketing: A Tear may be embedded into any finely crafted item made from an exotic material. Once socketed, it cannot be removed without shattering it.
  • Attunement: Each Tear requires individual attunement.
  • Limit: A single item may hold up to three Tears.
  • Resonance: Socketing two or more Tears of the same colour into a single item unlocks a set bonus.
  • Favoured Items: Each colour resonates more strongly with certain item types. On favoured items the set bonus is empowered; on non-favoured items it is lesser.
  • Affinity: Each colour governs specific damage/defence types. Whenever a feature grants extra damage or resistance, choose from that colour’s Affinity.
  • Wildcard Resonance (Black Tears): A Black Tear may count as any one colour to complete that colour’s set on its item. You declare the colour when you attune; once that item achieves Resonance with the Black Tear, its chosen colour cannot be changed.
  • Corruption: Attuning to more than three Tears total risks exhaustion, madness, or whispers of Uzume bleeding into the soul.

Colours of the Tears

Each Tear shines with a hue bound to one of Uzume’s final emotions. Affinity defines which damage types and defences that colour can grant.

  • Blue (Sorrow)

    • Favoured items: Defensive (armor, shields, helms)
    • Affinity: Lightning, Piercing
    • Theme: Loss, grief, endurance, protection

  • Red (Wrath)

    • Favoured items: Offensive (weapons, ammunition, staves, wands)
    • Affinity: Fire, Bludgeoning, Slashing, Piercing
    • Theme: Rage, destruction, divine fire

  • Gold (Joy)

    • Favoured items: Utility (cloaks, gloves, boots, amulets, rings)
    • Affinity: Radiant, Bludgeoning
    • Theme: Healing, courage, fellowship

  • Violet (Dream)

    • Favoured items: Utility (cloaks, rings, amulets) or Offensive (wands, staves)
    • Affinity: Psychic, Slashing
    • Theme: Illusion, trickery, bending of thought

  • Silver (Fate)

    • Favoured items: Any (versatile)
    • Affinity: Force, Thunder, Bludgeoning
    • Theme: Time, chance, inevitability

  • Black (Chaos)

    • Favoured items: Offensive & Defensive (weapons, armor)
    • Affinity: All damage types
    • Theme: Entropy, corruption, raw void

  • Green (Growth)

    • Favoured items: Offensive (weapons, ammunition) & Utility (cloaks, boots). *A Tear in ammunition makes that piece unbreakable.*
    • Affinity: Poison, Acid, Slashing
    • Theme: Renewal, venom, wild nature

  • White (Hope)

    • Favoured items: Defensive & Utility (armor, cloaks, amulets)
    • Affinity: Cold, Piercing
    • Theme: Purity, cleansing, divine shield

  • Grey (Memory)

    • Favoured items: Utility (rings, cloaks, amulets)
    • Affinity: Necrotic, Bludgeoning
    • Theme: Secrets, remembrance, echoes of the dead

When Resonance is achieved on a favoured item the set bonus is stronger. Affinity controls which damage types can appear as extra damage or resistances for that colour.

Using Tears in Play

  • A single Tear grants a minor boon.
  • A matched pair or trio turns an item into something unique and powerful. Two- and three-set bonuses are amplified when placed in favoured items.
  • A Black Tear can complete a colour set on that item (see Wildcard Resonance).
  • Pursuing full sets may require quests to track down matching colours.
  • Overuse tempts corruption. Beware the consequence of attuning to more than three gems.

Warnings

  • Irreversible: Once socketed, a Tear is bound to its item until destruction.
  • Limited Attunement: Each gem consumes one of your attunement slots.
  • Whispers of Uzume: Those who push beyond mortal limits may hear her sorrow bleed through.

Known Artefacts

Lightbringer

Weapon (shortsword), legendary (Requires Attunement)

Material Origin

  • Exotic Material: Mithral
  • Material Property — Swift Strike: Once per scene, reroll one damage die showing its minimum value.

Base Enchantment

This sword is a +3 shortsword (does not require attunement for the enchantment).

Socketed Tears of Uzume

Lightbringer holds three Red (Wrath) Tears of Uzume, socketed permanently into its hilt. Each Tear requires attunement.

Affinity of Wrath: Fire (primary)

  1. Uncommon — Tear of Fury.
    Effect: Once per long rest, add +2d6 fire damage to a melee attack.
  2. Rare — Tear of Burning Heart.
    Effect: Once per day, cast Fireball (3rd level).
  3. Very Rare — Tear of Bloodflame.
    Effect: Critical hits deal an additional 2d6 fire and necrotic damage.

Resonance Effects (Red Tears in a Weapon — Favoured Item)

  • 2-Gem Resonance (Favoured): Weapon attacks deal an additional +1d6 fire damage.
  • 3-Gem Resonance (Favoured): Once per long rest, unleash Uzume’s Inferno. As an action, Lightbringer belches forth a 30-foot cone of fire. Each creature in the area must make a DC 16 Dexterity saving throw, taking 6d8 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.

Description

Forged in Caratania’s hidden forges from pure mithral, Lightbringer burns with the wrath Uzume carried to her grave. Its edge gleams silver, but three crimson Tears pulse within the hilt like molten embers. Swift and deadly, the blade lashes out with searing flame, each strike echoing the grief of a slain goddess.

Aegis of the Weeping Sky

Armor (shield), very rare (Requires Attunement)

Material Origin

  • Exotic Material: Adamantine
  • Material Property — Unyielding: Once per scene, downgrade a critical hit against you to a normal hit.

Base Enchantment

This shield is a +2 shield (does not require attunement for the enchantment).

Socketed Tears of Uzume

Aegis holds three Blue (Sorrow) Tears, socketed permanently. Each Tear requires attunement.

Affinity of Sorrow: Lightning, Piercing

  1. Uncommon — Tear of Endurance.
    Effect: Gain +1 HP per character level.
  2. Rare — Tear of Mourning.
    Effect: Cast Calm Emotions once per long rest.
  3. Very Rare — Tear of the Weeping Sky.
    Effect: Gain resistance to psychic damage; allies within 10 ft have advantage on death saves.

Resonance Effects (Blue Tears in Armor — Favoured)

  • 2-Gem (Favoured): Gain resistance to one Blue Affinity type (Lightning or Piercing). Choose on attunement.
  • 3-Gem (Favoured): Once per long rest, negate a critical hit against you after it is rolled.

Description

Hammered from star-dark adamant, the Aegis drinks in thunder and sorrow alike. Blue Tears glimmer beneath the face of the shield like rain trapped in glass.

Briarstorm Longbow

Weapon (longbow), very rare (Requires Attunement)

Material Origin

  • Exotic Material: Netherbone
  • Material Property — Celestial Bane: On a critical hit against celestials, deal +2 of the weapon’s main damage type.

Base Enchantment

This is a +2 longbow (does not require attunement for the enchantment).

Socketed Tears of Uzume

The Briarstorm holds two Green (Growth) Tears and one Black (Chaos) Tear. The Black Tear is attuned to count as Green for this item’s set bonus (Wildcard Resonance). Each Tear requires attunement.

Affinity of Growth: Poison, Acid, Slashing   •   Affinity of Chaos: All damage types (for individual Black effects)

  1. Rare — Tear of Venom (Green).
    Effect: Weapon attacks deal an additional +1d6 poison damage.
  2. Uncommon — Tear of Renewal (Green).
    Effect: When you spend Hit Dice to heal, you may reroll 1s.
  3. Uncommon — Tear of Flux (Black).
    Effect: At dawn, gain resistance to a random damage type (roll).

Note: Because the Black Tear is declared Green for this item, it completes the 3-Gem Green set bonus on the bow.

Resonance Effects (Green Tears in Weapon — Favoured)

  • 2-Gem (Favoured): Weapon attacks deal +1d6 damage of a Green Affinity type (Poison, Acid, or Slashing).
  • 3-Gem (Favoured): Once per long rest, cast Wall of Thorns (save DC 16 or your spell save DC, whichever is higher).

A Green Tear socketed into ammunition makes that piece unbreakable.

Description

Vines knot the yew and never wither; each arrow loosed grows a whisper of leaves. In battle, the bow answers cruelty with overgrowth and thorn.
