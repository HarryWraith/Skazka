Ruins of Grimspire

Winter’s fallen citadel

Profile

Region Winter (north of the River Horn)
Founded 12
Origin Mountain citadel and trade keep
Environment High alpine; perpetual frost and gale exposure
Threat Level Very high (curse, undead, dragon sign)
Known Denizens Frostbitten spirits; rumoured Dark Walkers; Icethorn’s brood
Access Ice paths from Winter; via Winter’s Root mine tunnels
Superstitions “Fire dies at Grimspire; oaths freeze and break.”

Overview

Now a haunted husk, Grimspire’s halls are strewn with frozen wealth and watched by frost-numb dead. After hunters slew Glaclalis—the mate of the dragon Icethorn—her grief and wrath laid a lasting curse upon the citadel; some say the Dark Walkers still keep a sorrowed vigil while Icethorn broods upon the shattered towers.

Origin, Decline & Current state

Origin: Raised as a mountain stronghold over key passes, Grimspire served Winter as a trade keep and watch-citadel, its gates cut with oath-runes and its markets busy with alpine caravans.

Decline: The slaying of Glaclalis brought Icethorn’s vengeance. Night winds turned to razors, hoarfrost sealed doors, and the curse bound guardians where they stood—commerce ended in a breath of ice.

Current state: The fortress lies frost-locked and perilous. Smugglers reach it by Winter’s Root, but paths collapse and spirits grow colder the deeper one goes.

Places of Note

The Frost-Veined Gate

Granite threshold etched with oaths and betrayals; at dusk, frost seems to pulse from the stone like breath.

The Dark Walker’s Watch

A hidden tower carved into the rock; moonlight shows spectral blades where the last loyal sentries refused the hunt.

Icethorn’s Nesting Perch

A spiraled, shattered battlement where the dragon is said to brood—her silhouette sometimes cuts the pale moon.

Rumours

  • Icethorn circles the peak on thin moons; her shadow can freeze torchflame to glass.
  • The Dark Walkers grant safe passage to those who swear an oath—and keep it by dawn.
  • A bell beneath the Ghost Market tolls each midwinter for every hunter yet unpaid.

Loot myth

  • Glaclalis’ Tear: a frost-clear gem that snuffs all flames within 30 ft for one minute; warm hands suffer biting cold.
  • Oath-Vellum of the Watch: a strip of ice-cured hide that binds a spoken vow; break it and a Dark Walker comes to collect.
  • Market’s Pale Coin: a translucent token spendable only with the dead—redeem for one true answer, then it melts.

Dangers

  • Icethorn’s wrath: white squalls and strafing passes near upper towers.
  • Frostbound dead: spirits locked in the moment of the curse; numbing cold auras.
  • Breakable paths: corniced ice and snow bridges over chasms; collapse on vibration.
  • Oath-wards: runes that punish deceit with paralysis and ice-lash welts.
  • Smugglers’ traps: false markers and deadfalls along Winter’s Root.
