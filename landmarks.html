Glass Dunes

In eastern Kiajistan there is a region of desert that was the site of a magical battle so intense that it turned the sand to black glass. In 696 The Battle of the Dunes took place between opposing forces in first open battle of The Shadow War. Five hundred wizards and sorcorers through magic at each other for two days straight. It is rumoured both sides searched for an artefact.

The surface is so smooth here that it can take a whole day to move just a mile.
