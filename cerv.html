The Cerv Queen

Overview

The Cerv Dominion is a compact under-realm where caverns ring like drums and law travels on the air itself. Sapient insect clades—ants, beetles, mantids, hornets and their silk-spinning allies—live as one polity beneath a single brood-queen. Their cities are grown more than built: resin towers veined with amber, bridges braided from silk and chitin, and halls tuned to carry the soft hum of command.

All things are ordered by the Mandate, a pheromonal sovereignty that binds oath and ration, tribute and truce. Truth has a smell, perjury a taint. Outsiders are “solitaries” and receive chaperoned paths marked by scent-beacons and tremor-stones; hospitality is exact, gifts weighed against stores and wintering brood.

To the Cerv, memory is harvest. Ancestors are sealed in amber reliquaries and consulted in ritual clicks and vibrations. War is geometry—hex phalanxes in narrow throats, vespid dives in vaulted chambers—swift, precise, and finished before echoes settle. Those who keep their word will find the Dominion alien but navigable; those who break it will learn how quickly silk can become a shroud.

Not all is as it appears. Much of Cerv is alien - from its peoples to the structures, but visitors to this place cannot help but conclude much of the kingdom was built long ago and that the Cerv have merely redecorated. This place was made by hands that could not have been insectoid. Signs of an ancient civilisation are everywhere yet there is no documented hisotry. Who or what built this place?

Warrens & Hives

  • Resin Crown — royal district of amber spires and the Queen’s choir-organs.
  • Silk Span Quarter — bridges and wind-harps over the Black Chasm.
  • Amber Ledger — store-vaults, ration halls, and scent-scribe cloisters.

Factions & Orders

  • Resinwrights — termite-kin architects who cast living walls.
  • Silkwrights — spider allies weaving bridges, veils, and war-nets.
  • Pincers of War — Formicarii and Vesprial commanders of the Mandate.
“A kept oath sweetens the air.”

— Cerv proverb, taught to solitaries

Economy & Trade

  • Rare, formal exchanges: acid ampoules, biolume pearls, silk-chitin armor for iron, salt, grain.
  • Markets hum in warm resin halls; tools cure while bargains are struck and scented.

Rumours & hooks

  • A counterfeit Mandate (false scent) spreads in the Resin Bazaar.
  • Three royal pupae quicken; rival clades court the future.
  • Silk Span sings at night—some say the bridges remember footsteps.

Places of Note

The Amber Spire

A translucent resin tower veined with ancient inclusions. Within are sealed scent-relics— the Dominion’s memory distilled and kept for counsel.

The Broodwell Basilica

A living vault of silk veils and tremor-organs where the Queen resides. Commands here are sung through stone and bone.

The Silk Span

Suspension bridges stretched across a black chasm, lined with wind-harps. The cords thrumm warnings long before danger walks.

The Acid Falls

Mineral cascades diverted into amber-rimmed vats. The Vitreol Keepers guard the flow that feeds craft and war.

The Bone-Gardens

Ossuaries mulched with fungus-moss where names are sung as tremor hymns. The air is sweet with cedar resin.

Chittergate

A portcullis of interlocking mandibles that opens with a choir of clicks. Beyond lies a maze of switchback tunnels and scent-beacons.
