Character creation & advanacement

House rules

Character Creation

Ability Score Generation

  • All players roll six ability scores using 4d6, drop the lowest.
  • Each player generates a full array of six numbers.
  • The array with the highest total sum will be selected, and everyone at the table uses that same array.

Hit Points (HP)

At 1st level, characters begin with maximum hit points for their class.

Class & Race Options

Skazka is the dimensional crossroads of the multiverse.

All official and recognised third-party classes, subclasses, races, and lineages are available if presented during DnD Beyond character creation.

No restrictions based on setting.

Bonus Feat at 1st Level

Each character begins with one bonus feat at 1st level.

Exception: If your background or lineage already grants a feat, you do not gain an additional one.

Starting equipment

Choose standard character equipment.

Each player may choose one 'heirloom item' that matches their character backstory. It must not be legendary. Heriloom items are those made from an exotic material. A full list can be found here.

Level advanacement

Level advancement is achieved upon reaching milestones and only comes into effect upon the next long rest.

Hit Points (HP)

Upon reaching each new level HP are rolled. Players may reroll a 1 or 2 once. The reroll must be used.

Multiclassing

Multiclassing is permitted without any prerequisites or qualification.
