Character Creation

Ability Score Generation

All players roll six ability scores using 4d6, drop the lowest. Each player generates a full array of six numbers. The array with the highest total sum will be selected, and everyone at the table uses that same array. Hit Points (HP) At 1st level, characters begin with maximum hit points for their class. Class & Race Options Skazka is the dimensional crossroads of the multiverse. All official classes, subclasses, races, and lineages are available. No restrictions based on setting. Bonus Feat at 1st Level Each character begins with one bonus feat at 1st level. Exception: If your background or lineage already grants a feat, you do not gain an additional one.
