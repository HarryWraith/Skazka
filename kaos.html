Overview

Born from the depths of the abyss, Kaos is the living embodiment of entropy — the shadow to Uzume’s radiant light. Where she danced to inspire joy and harmony, he moves in silence, a hidden force pulling the threads of fate into knots. His presence is not felt, but known in the unraveling of order, in the collapse of certainty. The gambler who tempts fate, the warrior who trusts in luck, the lover who hopes for forever — all fall to the whisper of Kaos. His breath is the chill of inevitable ruin, a curse wrapped in chance. In his wake, patterns shatter, stars blink out, and the universe remembers its primal truth: that from chaos it was born, and to chaos it shall return.

Symbols

Ropes or braids unweaving on their own, seams splitting, ink feathering into chaotic tendrils, candles guttering without wind, and dice improbably landing askew.

Tenets

  • Court disorder—when structures calcify, break them so truth can breathe.
  • Embrace chance; accept loss and windfall alike without pleading for design.
  • Unravel false bonds: sever oaths forged in fear, expose patterns that pretend to be fate.

Rites

The Unbinding: Cut a braided cord into three and let the strands drift on running water; speak a vow you will willingly loosen.

Avatars

A tall, androgynous figure draped in a robe of loose, unraveling threads; where a face should be, there’s only a weave coming undone, revealing shifting darkness beneath.

Lore

A thread that unravels in front of your eyes is a sign that Kaos has his hand on events.
